GAME RULES:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

- In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he or she whishes. Each result get added to their -Current Score-

- BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all their -Current Score- gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that their -CurrentScore- gets added to their Big-score. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The first player to reach 100 points on Big-score wins the game. if we didn't specify the final score that is.
